

**HealthChain — Blockchain-Secured Medical Records System**

Healthcare in India runs on paperwork, and paperwork can be faked. A diagnosis can be changed, a bill can be inflated, and a patient can be denied a claim they genuinely deserve — all because there was no single source of truth anyone could trust. HealthChain was built to fix that.

For Hospitals — Every treatment record uploaded to HealthChain is permanently written to the blockchain. Nobody can go back and change it — not the hospital, not the insurer, not anyone. Ayushman Bharat integration means scheme eligibility is confirmed instantly, so hospitals spend less time on paperwork and more time on care.

For Patients— Your entire medical history, in one place, accessible anytime — without having to call a hospital and wait three days for a file. Your identity is verified through DigiLocker, so no more carrying physical documents everywhere. And if an insurer rejects your claim, you'll get a clear, human explanation of why — not a letter full of terms nobody understands.

For Insurance Companies— Every claim that comes in has already been scored for fraud risk on a scale of 0 to 100. The treatment record behind that claim sits on a blockchain that nobody has edited. You're not reviewing a document someone emailed you — you're looking at a record that has been untouched since the day it was created.

All files are encrypted before they ever leave the hospital's system. Every action leaves a permanent on-chain trail. Nothing is stored in a way that can be quietly modified later.

**HealthChain doesn't ask anyone to trust each other. It makes trust unnecessary.**
